Meet Our Experts

Fabio Violante

Chief Executive Officer

Working with Arduino since late 2015, and became CEO in 2017. Prior to joining Arduino, he co-founded enterprise software firm Neptuny, before selling it to BMC Software and acting as CTO for five years.

Massimo Banzi

Co-founder, Chairman & CMO

Interaction designer, educator, open-source hardware pioneer, and TED speaker. His background is in electrical engineering, but he spent most of his early career working as a software architect before spending four years at the Interaction Design Institute Ivrea as an Associate Professor.

Pietro Dore

Chief Operating Officer

Joined Arduino in 2014 following more than 15 years of executive experience at high-growth tech companies across Europe. Prior to that, he was a Senior Manager at Bain & Co. Pietro holds an MBA from Columbia Business School.
